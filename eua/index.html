Joker

Batman's classic comic book villain or Joker character has always caught people's attention, after all who is this clown-clad man who gives so much trouble to one of the most important Superheroes in the DC universe? Well, if you want to know more about this character, nothing better than an entire site on this subject. Let's talk here about your story, your character in comics and movie theaters.

A movie about him was recently released with the intention of making you bond with this character, making him even more famous. The film portrays the life of Arthur Fleck the Joker, a low-class man with a job that only gives enough for a living, in the course of the film is shown the suffering life of this character with various challenges in his daily life and facing various injustices to become the cruelest villain of all.

It's important to make it clear that it's a villain movie, and there's no middle ground to it, the movie is full of violence, it has scenes that really impact and make you doubt that it's really happening and in the end leave you with a smile on it. face with everything that is happening and still with a self pity of the character.

But that's not even half of the whole story, on the next pages there's a much more detailed summary not only about this movie, but about all the other along with the comic and also about the curse this character carries.
